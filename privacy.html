Privacy Policy

How information is collected, used, and protected on this website.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you visit this website.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect information in two ways:

Voluntary Information:

Personal information you provide through our “Contact Us” form (such as your name and email address). This is used solely to respond to your requests and is never sold or used for marketing.

Automated Information:

When you visit the site, certain information is collected automatically via Google Analytics.

2. Google Analytics and Cookies

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics uses “cookies,” which are small text files placed on your computer, to help analyze how users interact with the site.

The information generated by cookies (including your IP address) may be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google uses this information to:

  • Evaluate your use of the website
  • Compile reports on website activity
  • Provide services related to website usage

3. How to Opt-Out

You can prevent the use of cookies by adjusting your browser settings. However, some features of the site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Aside from data processed by Google Analytics:

  • Your personal information is never sold
  • It is never shared with outside organizations, except as required by law
  • It is never used for marketing or newsletters

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through the contact form and data collected via analytics from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal information submitted through the contact form.

Data collected through Google Analytics is typically aggregated and not stored in a way that personally identifies individuals.
