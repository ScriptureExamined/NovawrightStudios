About

NovaWright Studios

Intentional design. Thoughtful systems. Meaningful interactive experiences.

NovaWright Studios is led by Roberta Owen and built on a foundation of intentional design, thoughtful systems, and a commitment to creating meaningful interactive experiences.

This is not a studio driven by trends or volume. Each project is crafted with purpose—where story, mechanics, and atmosphere work together to engage the player and leave a lasting impact.

The goal is simple: create games that challenge perspective, reward attention, and stay with the player long after the experience ends.

That approach is shaped in part by Roberta’s work on Scripture Examined, where careful study, clarity, and a pursuit of truth guide the process. Those same principles carry into the studio’s development philosophy. Every element is deliberate, and nothing is included without reason.

NovaWright Studios is a long-term creative effort, built step by step, with clear direction and a focus on depth over noise.
