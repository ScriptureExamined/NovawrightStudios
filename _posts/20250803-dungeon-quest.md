Welcome to Dungeon Quest

Dungeon Quest is a game where your brain and your reflexes are put to the test. Inspired by the hypnotic movement of the Windows 95 Maze screensaver, it's more than a trip down memory lane, it’s a full-blown puzzle dungeon crawler with Sokoban-style mechanics reimagined for modern adventurers.
A Dungeon That Remembers You

In Dungeon Quest, the maze is not random. It’s generated with purpose, puzzles designed to challenge you, not frustrate you. You’ll push ancient blocks onto hidden switches, uncover secret paths, dodge deadly traps, and discover relics that slowly reveal the story of why you’re here... and who you are.

As in the best dungeon crawlers, what starts simple becomes layered: sliding floor tiles, rotating rooms, teleportation glyphs, and enemies that obey Sokoban logic but behave with cunning purpose.
Why We Built This

We wanted to merge the hypnotic, endless feel of the Microsoft Maze with the satisfying logic of block-pushing puzzles and the tension of dungeon exploration. There’s something deeply immersive about navigating spaces that almost make sense — where the walls feel alive and the floor beneath your feet can shift.
What Lies Ahead

We're crafting each layer of Dungeon Quest to make you feel lost, challenged, and curious. You’ll encounter thematic zones, from glitching maze halls to crumbling underground crypts. Each with its own mechanics and moods. There’s no hand-holding. You’ll need to think several steps ahead, like a chess master trapped in a labyrinth built by a mad architect.

But there’s always a way forward.
Ready to Enter?

Dungeon Quest isn’t about brute force. It’s about cleverness, timing, and embracing the strange. If you’ve ever been mesmerized by an old screensaver or lost hours to a puzzle game where every move matters, then this might just be your next obsession.
