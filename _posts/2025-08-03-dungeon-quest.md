# Dungeon Quest

Dungeon Quest is a game where your **brain and your reflexes** are put to the test.

Inspired by the hypnotic movement of the Windows 95 Maze screensaver, this is more than a trip down memory lane, it’s a full-blown puzzle dungeon crawler with Sokoban-style mechanics reimagined for modern players.

---

## A Dungeon That Remembers You

In Dungeon Quest, the maze is not random.

It’s generated with purpose—designed to challenge you, not frustrate you.

You will:

- push ancient blocks onto hidden switches
- uncover secret paths
- dodge deadly traps
- discover relics that slowly reveal the story

As in the best dungeon crawlers, what starts simple becomes layered:

- sliding floor tiles
- rotating rooms
- teleportation glyphs
- enemies that follow Sokoban logic but behave with intent

---

## Why We Built This

We wanted to merge:

- the hypnotic, endless feel of the Microsoft Maze
- the satisfying logic of block-pushing puzzles
- the tension of dungeon exploration

There’s something immersive about navigating spaces that almost make sense—where the walls feel alive and the floor beneath your feet can shift.

---

## What Lies Ahead

Dungeon Quest is being built to make you feel:

- lost
- challenged
- curious

You will explore themed zones such as:

- glitching maze corridors
- crumbling underground crypts

Each area introduces new mechanics and atmosphere.

There’s no hand-holding.

You will need to think several steps ahead—like a chess player trapped in a labyrinth designed by a mad architect.

But there is always a way forward.

---

## Ready to Enter?

Dungeon Quest isn’t about brute force.

It’s about:

- cleverness
- timing
- embracing the strange

If you’ve ever been mesmerized by an old screensaver or lost hours to a puzzle game where every move matters, this might be your next obsession.
